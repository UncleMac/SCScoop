GTM Portal

The Go-To Market Portal should always be the first place you look after a campaign launch. This portal is what the GTM team uses to post content, resources, and webcast recordings for campaigns. Through this portal, you can link to any additional resources you might need, both internal and customer-facing. Tip from the SCs: The GTM Portal shows info for campaigns across all of Oracle's LoBs. Check out the OD->Cloud->National page to see what campaigns your reps are running!

Link to the GTM Portal HERE.

Enablement Deck

As mentioned previously, the GTM portal is a vital resource. The first thing you should download from GTM is the enablement deck. This is the PowerPoint presentation that is given during the launch webcast for each campaign. To make it even easier, we put the direct download link in the Scoop. Tip from the SCs: Keep an eye out for any additional links to information sprinkled throughout the deck.

Find the download link for the ppt HERE.

Sales Central

After you've gotten familiar with the GTM Portal, the next place to visit is Sales Central. Just like the GTM Portal, Sales Central will have a separate page/section for every single campaign that is launched. You can use Sales Central to find assets and collateral to send to customers (like videos, whitepapers, datasheets and more). You can also find information that helps you draft emails and prep for cold calls. Tip from the SCs: Print out the BattleCards/CheatSheets from Sales Central and keep them at your desk.

Find this campaign's Sales Central page HERE

Learn more...

As always, one of the best places to find information is cloud.oracle.com. This is the customer-facing landing page that all our customers see when they research Oracle's Public Cloud. If a customer wants to read up on one of our services above and beyond the single datasheet you sent out, send them here. Tip from the SCs: My favorite link to suggest to a customer is the 'Learn more' tab for a specific cloud service.
